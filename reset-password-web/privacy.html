Privacy Policy

Last updated:

1) Controller and contact

This privacy notice explains how Umutima (“we”, “us”) processes personal data. For privacy requests, contact: omutimahelpcenter@gmail.com.

2) Data we collect

  • Account data: email, username, authentication/session data.
  • Profile data: name, age, gender, city/country, bio, interests, relationship preferences.
  • Photos: profile images you upload.
  • Messages: messages and pre-match messages you send.
  • Safety data: blocks and reports you submit.

3) Why we use data (purposes)

  • To create and manage your account and provide core app features (matching and messaging).
  • To keep the community safe (blocking/reporting, abuse prevention, and enforcement).
  • To improve app performance and reliability.

4) Legal basis

We process data to provide the service you request (account creation, profile display, matching, messaging). Where required, we rely on your consent (for example, optional communications). You can withdraw optional consent at any time by contacting us.

5) Sharing and service providers

We do not sell your personal data. We share data only with service providers required to operate the app. Examples:

  • Supabase: authentication, database, and server functions.
  • Cloudinary: image storage and delivery for profile photos.
  • Resend: sending certain transactional emails (for example data export email link).

6) International transfers

Depending on provider infrastructure, your data may be processed outside Rwanda. We use contractual and technical safeguards (such as access control and encryption in transit) to protect data.

7) Retention

  • We keep your profile and messages while your account is active.
  • If you delete your account, we delete your account data, except where we must retain limited information for legal compliance, fraud prevention, or safety.

8) Your rights

You can request:

  • Access to your data (export)
  • Correction of inaccurate data
  • Deletion of your account/data
  • Withdrawal of consent where applicable

To make a request, email omutimahelpcenter@gmail.com.

9) Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect data. No online service can guarantee absolute security.

10) Children

Umutima is for adults. You must be at least 18 years old to use the app.
