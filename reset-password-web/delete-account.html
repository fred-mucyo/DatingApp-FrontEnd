How to delete your account (in-app)

  1. Open the Mutima app and sign in.
  2. Go to Profile.
  3. Scroll to the bottom and tap Delete account.
  4. Confirm the deletion.

What is deleted

When you delete your account, we delete your account and profile data associated with your user ID.

What may be retained

We may retain limited information where required for legal compliance, fraud prevention, or safety reasons.

Need help?

If you cannot access your account and need deletion assistance, contact: privacy@mutima.app.
